Welcome to Audiolines!

Set the BPM!

You can set the BPM by clicking on the BPM at the top of the screen!

Click the metronome to toggle the BPM on/off!

Create your own recordings!

Tap the record icon to record a new track!

Your recordings are then visualized. Click on the three dots to enter the menu for that track.

In the menu, you can Mute the track(M), have it play Solo (S) or delete it entirely.

Export your track!

To save your recording, export the track to your phone(and share it)!
